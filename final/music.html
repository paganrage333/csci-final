about

Growing up I had brief stints in piano and drum lessons but what ultimately stuck

was the bass guitar around age thirteen. Within a year or so I was also learning

acoustic and electric guitar and started writng songs and playing with friends.

When I was seventeen I started Pictures of Vernon and when I was eighteen we

went on our first tour. In 2017 we did our first full US tour, including Canada.

That year also saw our release of a split 10" with Prince Daddy & the Hyena and

Mom Jeans entitled Now That's What I Call Music Vol. 420. In 2018 we continued

touring the US and additionally toured Mexico, a country south of America.

Also in 2018, I formed the band Diva Sweetly with some friends. We released

a few singles that year and in January 2019 we put out our debut album,

In The Living Room which received a 6.9 from Pitchfork. In 2020, Pictures of

Vernon put out our debut album, bug, through Counter Intuitive Records.

In 2021 I joined the band Save Face and embarked on several US,

Canadian, and UK tours. At the end of 2022, after playing over one

hundred shows that year with Save Face and over five shows with Pictures of

Vernon, I decided to step back from the rigorous touring schedule and go back to

school. In 2023, I began to work on this website and in 2023 I completed it.
